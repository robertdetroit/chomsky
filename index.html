About Me

Hi there, I'm Robbie Ha, a senior at Duke University from Seattle, WA! I am pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science, a B.A. in Public Policy, and a minor in Political Science. As an engineer, I'm interested in the applications of machine learning on the social sciences.


On campus, I am involved in residence life (I'm on my third year as a resident assistant for first year students!) and am a member of the Editorial Board of The Duke Chronicle, The Thompson Writing Program, and the Undergraduate Conduct Board.


Outside of my studies, I love quality journalism, hoppy beer, and adding more parentheses to my sentences than I need.


Currently splitting time between Raleigh-Durham, Seattle, and New York City.
